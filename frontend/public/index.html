WHAT IT DOES

Every tool your
token project needs

Stop duct-taping five different tools together. Token OS gives your project a single ops layer from day one.

◉
LIVE HOLDER ANALYTICS
Real-time holder count from Helius RPC. Top 50 wallets ranked by size, growth chart updated hourly, full history stored.
🐋
WHALE ALERTS
Helius webhooks catch every large transfer the moment it hits chain. Your Telegram group gets pinged automatically — no polling, no delay.

MILESTONE ALERTS
Set holder count or price targets. When they hit, your community gets an announcement automatically — no manual posting.

SETUP IN MINUTES

Why we built
Token OS

Token OS was built because every Solana token dev was juggling five different browser tabs — one for holder counts, one for price, a spreadsheet for airdrops, a bot template from GitHub, and a Telegram group going crazy with no automation.

We collapsed all of it into one platform. One Railway deploy gives you live holder analytics from Helius, price from Jupiter, milestone alerts, an airdrop distribution tool, and a full Telegram bot — all wired together and ready in under 20 minutes.

Built by Solana devs who've shipped tokens, bots, and launchpads. We know what actually matters at launch: your community seeing traction in real time, whales being called out instantly, and milestones celebrated automatically.

FAQ

Common questions

All on-chain data comes from Helius — holder counts use their getTokenAccounts RPC method, and live transfer events use Helius webhooks. Price data comes from the Jupiter Price API v2.
Basic comfort with GitHub and Railway is all you need. Push three folders to repos, set environment variables, and everything auto-deploys. Most devs are live in under 20 minutes.
When you execute an airdrop, 1% of the total SOL is automatically routed to the FEE_WALLET address you configure during setup. The preview screen shows the exact fee before you commit. Studio plan drops this to 0.5%.
Yes. Any SPL token on Solana mainnet works — paste the mint address and Token OS pulls holders, metadata, and price. Pump.fun tokens, Raydium-listed tokens, and custom launches all work the same way.
Snapshots run hourly on Builder and Studio plans, every 6 hours on Trial. You can trigger a fresh snapshot manually from the dashboard at any time. Whale alerts fire in near-real-time via Helius webhooks.
White-label is available on the Studio plan. You get the full source, can rebrand with your token's name and colors, and host it at your own domain. Contact us after signup.
Railway Hobby plan is $5/month and covers all three services (backend, bot, Postgres). Helius free tier covers ~300 projects. Vercel frontend is free. Total infra cost is about $5/month.
